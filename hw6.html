Objects

Homework 6 contains 3 required programs, working on Math object, regular expressions, masking, named capture groups, and open a new window along with document write.

HW6 Part 1

Viewing a number with 4 decimals or more as whole number, square-root with whole number, rounding to tenth, hundredth and thousandth.

HW6 Part 2

Display total matched found in current window. Otherwise, display searched character not found in a new window of 300 pixels by 100 pixels.

HW6 Part 3

Takes the provided phone number and displays it without hyphen nor parentheses. Script will use masking, and named capture grouping.
