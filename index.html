Author's Background

My name is Angel Chung. My family and I immigrated to the United States when I was 11 years old. I learned to not only speak English, but comprehend mathematics from the ground up. I graduated from the San Francisco public High School system with Advanced Placement in Calculus. Afterwards, I attended San Francisco State University and earned a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

Work Life

My professional career began as a Quality Assurance Engineer with Evolve Software. My experience began with this firm in White Box Testing, and progressed into Black Box Testing. This experience provided valuable technical and testing skills. Ultimately, I was placed in charge of server setup and installation. Additional responsibilities included database management and Perforce administration. Moving forward from Evolve Software, the next chapter of my career began with Moody's Analytics. My skill set developed into project management and offshore resources, as well as executing test cases and planning. Furthermore, my experience at this firm enabled the opportunity to manage Web API and UI automations alongside manual testing.
Currently, I provide IT support in addition to vendor development assistance with The City and County of San Francisco. My primary role is to merge features and functionality of a much needed upgrade from the past, into an entirely new CMS system with modern features, better performance and enhanced usability.

The ME Time

Alongside family and profession, my physical and mental well being is managed through an early morning session of yoga and running. My evening routine consists of continuing education before a dedicated window of entertainment before bed.
